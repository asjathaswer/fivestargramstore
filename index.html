Frequently Asked Questions ❓
How do I place an order? ▼
Simply add items to your cart, click "Order via WhatsApp", fill in your details, and hit send! Your order message will be sent directly to our WhatsApp. We'll confirm your order and delivery details within minutes.
What areas do you deliver to? ▼
We currently deliver across Colombo and suburbs. Delivery charges vary by location and will be confirmed via WhatsApp after you place your order. Pickup is also available from our Pettah store.
What is the minimum order for wholesale? ▼
Wholesale orders start from 5+ pieces per item. Our wholesale section shows bulk pricing for larger quantities. Contact us via WhatsApp for custom bulk pricing on very large orders.
How can I pay for my order? ▼
We accept bank transfers (Commercial Bank, Sampath Bank, HNB), cash on delivery, and cash on pickup. After bank transfer, please send your receipt via WhatsApp to confirm your order.
Are products fresh and quality guaranteed? ▼
Absolutely! All our products are sourced fresh and stored properly. We guarantee the quality of every item we sell. If you're unsatisfied with your purchase, contact us immediately via WhatsApp and we'll make it right.
